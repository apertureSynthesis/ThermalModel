Pipeline for generating thermal models of asteroids for comparison against observations. 
Uses KRC model inputs to generate radiative temperature profiles, 
which are then converted to surface emission at a given frequency. 

Many of the routines are adapted from Drs. Jian-Yang Li and Xiao-Duan Zou.
See Li, Y.-J. et al. 2026, "Thermal and Dielectric Properties of Juno's Regolith
at 1 Millimeter Wavelength", Planetary Science Journal, 7, 721,
doi: 10.3847/PSJ/ae468f
